Goal

My goal with this portfolio is to create a mordern looking portfolio useful for job applications and the presentation of my works.

Sources

For my font, I'm using Open Sans by Steve Matteson from Google Fonts.

Design

My current portfolio spans 60% of the viewport width, and I decided to use 220px wide images to account for this. When the screen shrinks below 1200, 900, and 600px in width, my design changes to span 70%, 80%, and 85% of the vw respectively in order to implement responsive design. The images also grow whenever a breakpoint is reached [to 250, 280, 310px], and wrap rows so all images will always be visible on the screen. In addition, the gallery images are centered so they look in place even when there are only 1 or 2 out of the total 3 gallery images on screen.
