Edifice of Fiends

Role : Independent Developer
Platforms : Steam, Windows
Engine : RPGMaker MV
Development Time : 4 Months (November 2020 - February 2021)
Genres : Singleplayer, Roguelike, Strategy RPG

Game Info

Edifice of Fiends is a challenging Singleplayer Roguelike RPG where every choice matters. The goal is to conquer each floor of the Edifice and climb to the top.
There are over 15 different skills that the player can learn and over 40 different enemies that can spawn.
Players grow stronger by purchasing items and distributing stat points they gain. The game contains over 100 unique items that are randomly available, which makes each playthrough a fresh experience.

My Experience

I learned a lot from developing this game such as scope management, level design, and enemy design [over 40 unique enemies]. After publishing the game and watching players play, I was able to get a better understanding of how to design enemies that are interesting and challenging obstacles to players.
The biggest challenge I faced was finishing and publishing the game. Since this was the first game I've ever published, I had trouble figuring out when to end the development process and get the build onto Steam. However, I did figure out both when to finish and how to publish, and the game was released shortly after.
